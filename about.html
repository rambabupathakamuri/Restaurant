About Us

Indian Food Palace has been serving authentic Indian cuisine in Boston since 1998. Our mission is to bring the rich and diverse flavors of India to our community, using only the freshest ingredients and traditional cooking methods.

Our team of chefs is passionate about creating dishes that not only taste great but also represent the cultural heritage of India. Whether you're a fan of spicy curries, flavorful biryanis, or sweet desserts, you'll find something to love on our menu.

We believe in providing a warm and welcoming atmosphere for our guests, making every visit a memorable experience.
